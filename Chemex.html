How to make Pour Over Coffee




Ingredients:

  • Chemex
  • Gooseneck kettle
  • Coffee Beans
  • Measuring scale
  • 800 grams of boiling water
  • Paper cone coffee filter
  • Coffee grinder



Procedure:

  • Put filter paper on the Chemex

  • Rinse the filter with boiling water

  • Drain out the water from Chemex




  • Grind 40 grams of coffee beans to a medium-fine level




  • Add the ground coffee onto the Chemex

  • Pre-wet the coffee for 30-45 seconds

  • Slowly pour water over the filter in a circle




  • Once the brewing is complete we can ditch the filter

  • Let it sit for a minute

  • Finally we'll have our delicious cup of coffee
